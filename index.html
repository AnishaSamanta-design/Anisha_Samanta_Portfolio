02 — ABOUT

Engineer by degree.
Designer by choice.

❧

I started my career in enterprise IT, working in asset management and security operations before transitioning into UI/UX design.

Those early years gave me firsthand exposure to operational workflows, system dependencies, and the everyday challenges users face while working with complex tools. Working closely with users in support-driven environments helped me develop empathy, active listening, and a deeper understanding of user-centered problem solving — perspectives that continue to shape my design approach today.

Over the past three years as a UI/UX designer, I've designed 50+ experiences across healthcare, fintech, banking, transport, hospitality and other domains. My work includes enterprise products and business demos for renowned clients across industries, where I focused on simplifying complex workflows into intuitive experiences while maintaining brand guidelines, design consistency, accessibility, and business requirements.
